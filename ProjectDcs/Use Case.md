## UC-FA-OB-001: Onboarding Checklist
**Actor**: HR (FA User)

**FA-Specific Flow**:
1. New hire created in ksf_FA_HRM
2. FA shows onboarding checklist:
   - HR tasks (paperwork)
   - IT tasks (equipment, accounts)
   - Manager tasks
3. Track completion in FA
